How it works

From customer click to checked-in player

A walkthrough of the journey a booking takes through TargetBooker, end to end.

Step 1

Customer books online

Your venue gets a branded booking page (e.g. app.targetbooker.com/your-venue) showing live availability for every activity and session time. Customers pick a slot, enter group size, and pay or leave a deposit through Stripe — no phone call needed.

Step 2

Confirmation goes out instantly

The moment payment clears, an automated confirmation email is sent from your venue's address with the booking details. Your team sees the new booking land in the admin dashboard immediately.

Step 3

Waivers get signed before arrival

Each participant receives a link to complete and sign a digital disclaimer ahead of their visit. Signed waivers are stored against the booking, so reception can check who's covered at a glance instead of handing out clipboards.

Step 4

Reminders keep people showing up

An automated reminder email goes out ahead of the session, cutting down on no-shows without anyone on your team having to send it manually. Beyond reminders, fully automated emails can be created directly from the dashboard to send out any important information — closures, weather alerts, offers or anything else your venue needs customers to know.

Step 5

Your team runs the day from the dashboard

On the day, staff use the admin dashboard to see who's booked in, check waiver status, manage walk-ins, and adjust or refund bookings if plans change.

Step 6

Every customer is remembered

After the visit, the customer's record stays in TargetBooker — booking history, participants, waivers — so the next time they book, your team already knows who they are.
