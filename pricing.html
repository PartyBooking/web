FAQ

Pricing questions

Is there a setup fee? +

No. There are no setup fees on any plan — you can be live and taking bookings within a week of signing up.

Do you take a cut of my bookings? +

No. TargetBooker charges a flat monthly fee per venue. Payments go through your own Stripe account, so you keep what you earn (minus standard Stripe processing fees).

Can I cancel at any time? +

Yes. There are no long-term contracts — every plan can be cancelled at any time.

What happens if I go over my plan's participant limit? +

You're not cut off — extra participants are simply billed at your plan's overage rate (e.g. £0.40/participant on Standard, down to £0.18 on Premium). Enterprise has no limit at all.

Do all plans get the same features? +

Yes. Every feature — booking, payments, waivers, accounting, discount codes, gift cards, email automations and custom branding — is available on every plan. Plans only differ by usage limits (participants, activities, emails and storage).

Do you offer a free trial? +

Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial — no credit card required to start.
