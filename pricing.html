FAQ

Pricing questions

Is there a setup fee? +

No. There are no setup fees on any plan — you can be live and taking bookings within a week of signing up.

Do you take a cut of my bookings? +

No. TargetBooker charges a flat monthly fee per venue. Payments go through your own Stripe account, so you keep what you earn (minus standard Stripe processing fees).

Can I cancel at any time? +

Yes. There are no long-term contracts — monthly plans can be cancelled at any time, and annual plans renew only with your confirmation.

What counts as a "venue"? +

A venue is a single physical location with its own booking page. If you run multiple activities (e.g. laser tag and an escape room) at the same address, that's one venue.

Do you offer a free trial? +

Yes. Sign up and start setting up your venue free — you won't be charged until you're ready to go live.
