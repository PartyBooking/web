Platform

Every part of the booking journey, handled

From the moment a customer finds your venue to the moment they walk out the door — TargetBooker covers it.

Smart scheduling

Set your session times, capacity and age restrictions — customers only ever see what's actually available. No double-booking, no back-and-forth.

Flexible payments

Take deposits or full payment at the time of booking. Stripe integration is built in, with funds settling directly to your own account.

Multi-activity

Run laser tag, paintball and archery all from one dashboard, each with its own schedule, pricing and session rules.

Packages & pricing

Flat-rate party packages or per-player pricing — or run both side by side, with custom extras your customers can add at checkout.

Month-by-month accounting

Revenue, collected vs. outstanding, and breakdowns by game type. Click into any month to drill straight into the detail.

Participant database

Collect dates of birth and contact emails per player, export to CSV, and have birthday reminders built right in.

Everything, on every plan

No feature gating — every plan gets the full platform

Plans only differ by usage limits (participants, activities, emails, storage). Every venue gets every feature below, from day one.

— Online booking & calendar — Payments & deposits — Customer accounts & dashboard — Disclaimers / waivers — Custom extras & packages — Age-restricted sessions
— Accounting & reporting — Discount codes — Gift cards — Email automations (reminders, birthday mailshots, party invites) — Custom branding (logo, colours, top bar)
