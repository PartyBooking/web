Platform

Every part of the booking journey, handled

From the moment a customer finds your venue to the moment they walk out the door — TargetBooker covers it.

Online booking

A clean, mobile-first booking page for every venue: live session availability, group sizes and add-ons. Customers book in under a minute without calling or emailing your team.

  • — Real-time availability, no double-booking
  • — Custom session times and group sizes per activity
  • — Works on any device, no app to download

Digital waivers & disclaimers

Participants complete and sign liability waivers online before they ever set foot in your venue. Every signed disclaimer is stored against the booking and searchable for compliance.

  • — Sent automatically after booking
  • — One per participant, fully tracked
  • — No more clipboards at reception

Payments & deposits

Secure online payments powered by Stripe. Take full payment or a deposit at the time of booking, cutting down no-shows and protecting your revenue.

  • — Card payments, Apple Pay & Google Pay
  • — Refunds and adjustments from the dashboard
  • — Funds settle directly to your own Stripe account

Automated emails

Booking confirmations, waiver requests and reminder emails are sent automatically, branded with your venue's name — no manual chasing required.

  • — Instant confirmation on every booking
  • — Automatic reminders ahead of the session
  • — Reliable delivery, no spam folder issues

Admin dashboard

A single screen built for the pace of a real venue: today's sessions, who's checked in, revenue collected and what's left outstanding.

  • — Daily session and booking overview
  • — Edit, cancel or refund bookings on the spot
  • — Revenue tracking, no spreadsheets needed

Customer & participant records

Every customer, every visit and every participant's waiver history lives in one searchable place — so your team always knows who's coming and when they last visited.

  • — Full booking history per customer
  • — Searchable by name, email or phone
  • — Repeat-customer visibility for your team
Multi-venue

Running more than one location? One account, every venue.

Each venue gets its own branded booking page, sessions and pricing, while you manage all of them from one TargetBooker account.
